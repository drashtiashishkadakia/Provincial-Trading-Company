About Us

Mumbai's hardware backbone, since 1963.

Provincial Trading Co. has been a trusted name in Mumbai's hardware and fastener trade for over 63 Years. Our shop is based in Nagdevi, one of Mumbai's oldest and most respected hardware markets, and we supply contractors, fabricators, electricians, plumbers and manufacturers across the country.

We have built this business on three decades of straight dealing. Right products, fair prices and on time delivery. Whether you walk into our shop in Mumbai or place a bulk order from anywhere in India, you will always get the same quality and service.
